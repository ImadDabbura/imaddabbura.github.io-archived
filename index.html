Biography

Imad Dabbura is a Data Scientist at Baylor Scott and White Health. He has many years of experience in predictive analytics where he worked in a variety of industries such as Consumer Goods, Real Estate, Marketing, and Healthcare.

Among other things, Imad is interested in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. He writes articles related to machine learning and AI on Medium and contributes to open source such as publishing his educational notebooks on github.
